About Community Health Bridge

An educational initiative focused on accessible health literacy for students in grades 4–8.

Our programs use engaging lessons, demonstrations, and discussions to introduce foundational medical and biological topics. We believe every student deserves the opportunity to understand how their body works and how to make healthy choices.

🩺

Interactive Health Workshops

Structured sessions introducing students to basic health and science concepts through live demonstrations and collaborative discussion.

🥦

Preventive Health Education

Students learn essential topics emphasizing prevention and long-term well-being through interactive activities.

Nutrition Sleep Exercise Hygiene
🏫

School & Community Partnerships

CHB collaborates with schools, libraries, and youth organizations to make health education accessible to as many students as possible.

🔭

Youth Medical Exploration

Students gain exposure to healthcare careers and real-world scientific applications, opening doors to future pathways in medicine and science.
